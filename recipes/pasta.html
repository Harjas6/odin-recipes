Pasta Recipe

Description

This is a recipe about how to make pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cream
  • Onions
  • Chicken
  • Parmesan
  • Cajun Seasoning
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cook pasta until al dente while doing the following steps
  2. Put some oil in a bowl witht he chicken then coat chicken in cajun seasoning
  3. Cook chicken in pan
  4. Take chicken out and in the same pan put dcied onions and cook until translucent. Then put cream in with parmesan and cajun seasoning
  5. Let cream simmer until prefered thickness then add chicken and pasta and stir
