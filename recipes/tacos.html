Taco Recipe

Description

Ground beef tacos go crazy

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onions and Tomatos
  • Sour cream
  • Lime
  • Avacodo
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Cilantro and jalepeno

Steps

  1. Cook Ground beef in pan
  2. Dice onions, Tomatoes, and jalepeno
  3. Mix them in a bowl and add salt and pepper, as well as a little bit of lime
  4. Cut avacsoado and smah them up. Add a spoon of sourcream and mix. Then add salt to liking
  5. BOOM done.
