Chicken Curry Recipe

Description

This is a recipe about how to make chicken curry. Chicken curry is an indian dish that is easy to make and flavourful

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onion and Tomato
  • Garlic and Ginger
  • Garam Masala
  • Meat Masala
  • Cumin powder and Coriander powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut onions into small peices or use a machine to chop them up
  2. Mince the garlic and cut the ginger into small peices
  3. Do the same for the tomatos
  4. Add the onions into the pan and fry until they are translucent then add the garlic,ginger and tomatos. Stir until it becomes paste-like
  5. Now add the chicken in whatver sized peices you like.
  6. Cook on medium high for 3 minutes and then se to medium and cover
  7. Once chicken is cooked add water and evaporate it as you please to dictate the level of curry/dryness
